Potato Bread I Recipe

Ingredients
1. ¾ cup water
2. ⅔ cup instant mashed potato flakes
3. 1 egg
4. 2 tablespoons margarine
5. 2 tablespoons white sugar
6. ¼ cup dry milk powder
7. 1 teaspoon salt
8. 3 cups bread flour
9. 1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast

Steps
1. Add ingredients to bread machine in order suggested by the manufacturer.   Add enough water to make a firm dough; use less if leftover potatoes are used.
2. Bake at regular bread cycle.
