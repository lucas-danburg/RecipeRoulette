Pumpernickel Bread I Recipe

Ingredients
1. 1 ½ cups warm milk
2. 1 ½ tablespoons vegetable oil
3. 3 tablespoons molasses
4. 2 ½ cups bread flour
5. 1 cup rye flour
6. ⅓ cup cornmeal
7. 1 teaspoon salt
8. 1 ¾ teaspoons active dry yeast
9. 3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps
1. Place ingredients into the bread machine in order suggested by the manufacturer.
2. Use the Light Crust or Whole Wheat settings.  Start the machine.
