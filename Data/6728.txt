Quick Lemon Poppy Seed Bread Recipe

Ingredients
1. 1 (18.25 ounce) package lemon cake mix
2. 4 eggs
3. ½ cup vegetable oil
4. 1 (3 ounce) package instant lemon pudding mix
5. 1 cup water
6. ¼ cup poppy seeds

Steps
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour 2 - 8x4 inch loaf pans.
2. In a large bowl, mix together the cake mix, eggs, oil, pudding mix, water, and poppy seeds.  Spread batter into two greased 8x4 inch loaf pans.
3. Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the cake comes out clean.
