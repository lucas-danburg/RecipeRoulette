Sun Dried Tomato and Asiago Cheese Bread Recipe

Ingredients
1. 1 cup water
2. 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
3. 1 teaspoon lemon juice
4. 2 tablespoons white sugar
5. 2 tablespoons instant powdered milk
6. 1 teaspoon salt
7. 2 ½ cups bread flour
8. ½ cup amaranth flour
9. 2 tablespoons chopped sun-dried tomatoes
10. ¼ cup grated Asiago cheese
11. 1 teaspoon dried basil
12. ½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
13. 1 teaspoon active dry yeast

Steps
1. Place all ingredients into bread machine in order given.
2. Bake according to bread machine directions for regular bake.
