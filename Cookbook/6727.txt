Mom&#x27;s Hazelnut Special Recipe

Ingredients
1. ½ tablespoon active dry yeast
2. 1 ¼ cups bread flour
3. 1 cup whole wheat flour
4. 1 tablespoon bread flour
5. 1 ⅛ cups warm water (110 degrees F/45 degrees C)
6. 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
7. 1 ½ tablespoons sunflower seeds
8. 2 cups chopped hazelnuts
9. 1 tablespoon white sugar
10. 1 ½ tablespoons vegetable oil
11. ⅔ teaspoon salt

Steps
1. Follow the directions for your bread machine, but do not reserve the nuts for later: add them at the beginning for better flavor.
