Cinnamon Cranraisin Bread Recipe

Ingredients
1. ¾ cup warm water
2. 1 egg, lightly beaten
3. 1 tablespoon margarine
4. 2 ⅔ cups all-purpose flour
5. 3 tablespoons dry milk powder
6. 2 tablespoons white sugar
7. 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
8. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
9. 1 teaspoon salt
10. 1 ½ teaspoons active dry yeast
11. ½ cup sweetened dried cranberries

Steps
1. Place warm water, egg, butter or margarine, flour, powdered milk, sugar, cinnamon, vanilla, salt, and yeast into the bread machine in the order suggested by the manufacturer. Start the machine.
2. After the first rise, add the dried cranberries.  Continue baking.
