Chocolate Chip Pumpkin Bread Recipe

Ingredients
1. 3 cups white sugar
2. 1 (15 ounce) can pumpkin puree
3. 1 cup vegetable oil
4. ⅔ cup water
5. 4 eggs
6. 3 ½ cups all-purpose flour
7. 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
8. 1 tablespoon ground nutmeg
9. 2 teaspoons baking soda
10. 1 ½ teaspoons salt
11. 1 cup miniature semisweet chocolate chips
12. ½ cup chopped walnuts

Steps
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour three 1 pound size coffee cans, or three 9x5 inch loaf pans.
2. In a large bowl, combine sugar, pumpkin, oil, water, and eggs.  Beat until smooth.  Blend in flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, baking soda, and salt.  Fold in chocolate chips and nuts. Fill cans 1/2 to 3/4 full.
3. Bake for 1 hour, or until an inserted knife comes out clean.  Cool on wire racks before removing from cans or pans.
